The Textbook Formula

$$A = P \left[ \frac{1 - \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{-nt}}{\frac{r}{n}} \right]$$
A = Loan Amount The present value or starting principal.
P = Payment The recurring payment per period.
r = Annual Rate The yearly interest rate (entered as % in the form).
n = Payments/Year Frequency of compounding and payments.
t = Time (Years) The total duration of the loan. The total number of payments equals n × t.
